Basic important points to keep in mind to set preventive measures and also avoid further spread of (COVID-19) / Corona virus.
  • 1. Regularly wash your hands with and alcohol-based rub or wash it with soap and water.
  • 2. Maintain at least 3 metres of distance between you and anyone who is coughing or sneezing.
  • 3. Don't touch your eyes, nose or mouth frequently. Virus can be transferred easily if the hands are contaminated.
  • 4. Practicing a good respiratory hygiene is a must. Cover your mouth while sneezing or coughing.
  • 5. If you are having cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical care immediately.
  • 6. Keep in mind about the virus prevention guidelines set by your government or local authority.
  • 7. Avoid travelling to places which are affected by corona virus outbreak and keep in mind about the travel advisory set by the local body.
  • 8. Eat healthy, hygienic & well cooked food.
  • 9. Refer WHO website for further info by clicking here
